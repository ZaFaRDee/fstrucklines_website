About Us

Learn more about F&S TRUCKLINES LLC

Transportation Insight Holding Company (TI) is the combination of industry-leading logistics providers Transportation Insight and Nolan Transportation Group (NTG). TI brings over two decades of multimodal expertise and technology to the logistics industry and ranks among North America’s top logistics companies. TI services more than 14,000 shippers and over 80,000 carriers through its proprietary Beon™ Digital Logistics Platform – a single point of access to TI’s mode-agnostic network and services from port to porch. The TI services and digital product portfolio spans North America, offering domestic freight and parcel transportation solutions, warehousing, data intelligence and supply chain consulting.
